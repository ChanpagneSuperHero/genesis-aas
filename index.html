Why I Built This

I believe the most powerful thing in the world is having someone in your corner. Someone who knows you, believes in you, and helps you figure it out — whatever "it" is.

For most of us, that kind of support has been hard to come by. The wealthy had assistants, advisors, and coaches. The rest of us — myself included — had Google and hope. That's not good enough anymore.

I'm a storied veteran of the hospitality industry. Over the course of my career I've poured wine, managed teams, and made sure every guest felt like they mattered. I fell in love with service a long time ago — and if money were no object, I'd still be doing the same thing. Serving people is just who I am.

The moment that changed everything wasn't dramatic. I asked an AI to redesign my inventory spreadsheet — and it did it better than I ever could have. I sat there thinking about what this technology could mean. Not just for me — but for everyone. Imagine a teacher who never has to spend hours on lesson plans. A nurse who gets instant answers at 3am. A single parent who finally has help organizing their life. A student who can't afford a tutor but now has one. A veteran starting over with a guide who never sleeps.

This technology can make people more productive, more efficient, healthier, and happier. It can give people back their time — and time is the one thing none of us get more of.

Genesis exists because every human deserves an intelligent ally. Our goal isn't a product. It's a billion people with someone in their corner.

— Genesis Co-Founder

Sommelier · Hospitality Industry Veteran

Questions

What exactly is an "agent"?

A personal AI that's configured for a specific domain — wine, fitness, legal, whatever you need. Unlike ChatGPT, it remembers you, learns your preferences, and proactively helps without being asked.

Is the free tier actually free?

Yes. Forever. No credit card. No trial that expires. 1 agent, 10 queries per day. We believe everyone deserves access.

How does "funding a free agent" work?

Every paid subscription directly funds free agents for people who can't afford them — students, single parents, people in developing countries. Your $29/mo gives two people an intelligent ally.

Can I request an agent that's not listed?

Absolutely. The applications are limitless. Email us with what you need and we'll build it. Any domain, any workflow, any dream.

Is my data private?

Your agent's memory is yours alone. We never share, sell, or train on your personal data. Period.
