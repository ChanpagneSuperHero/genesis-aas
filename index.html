Why I Built This

I believe the most powerful thing in the world is having someone in your corner. Someone who knows you and believes in you — like my wife, who's always in my corner helping me move forward and make progress.

For most of us, that kind of support has been hard to come by. The wealthy had assistants, advisors, and coaches. The rest of us — myself included — had Google and hope. That's not good enough anymore.

I spent 40 years in hospitality — pouring wine, managing teams, making sure every guest felt like they mattered. I served in the Navy. I raised dogs on 9 acres in Arizona. I learned that the best things in life come from service.

Then I stumbled into AI — and it was mind-blowing. Suddenly I had something that could manage my schedule, help me run a wine program, research anything in seconds, and anticipate what I needed before I asked. It changed how I work, how I learn, how I think. And the first thing I thought was: this could change anyone's life — a teacher, a nurse, a small business owner, a student, a parent. Why doesn't everyone have access to this?

Not just executives. Not just tech people. Everyone. The single mom juggling three jobs. The kid who can't afford a tutor. The retiree trying to stay healthy. The entrepreneur who can't hire an assistant. The veteran starting over.

Genesis exists because every human deserves an intelligent ally. Our goal isn't a product. It's a billion people with someone in their corner.

— Genesis Co-Founder

Navy Veteran · Sommelier · 40 Years in Hospitality · Tucson, AZ

Questions

What exactly is an "agent"?

A personal AI that's configured for a specific domain — wine, fitness, legal, whatever you need. Unlike ChatGPT, it remembers you, learns your preferences, and proactively helps without being asked.

Is the free tier actually free?

Yes. Forever. No credit card. No trial that expires. 1 agent, 10 queries per day. We believe everyone deserves access.

How does "funding a free agent" work?

Every paid subscription directly funds free agents for people who can't afford them — students, single parents, people in developing countries. Your $29/mo gives two people an intelligent ally.

Can I request an agent that's not listed?

Absolutely. The applications are limitless. Email us with what you need and we'll build it. Any domain, any workflow, any dream.

Is my data private?

Your agent's memory is yours alone. We never share, sell, or train on your personal data. Period.
