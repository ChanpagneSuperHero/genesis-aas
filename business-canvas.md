# Project Genesis AaS MVP - Business Model Canvas

## Key Partners
- OpenClaw platform / AI models (Grok, Claude)
- Hosting providers (Netlify/Vercel for landing, AWS/Heroku for agents)
- Payment processors (Stripe)
- Freelance developers for custom agent builds
- Social platforms (X, LinkedIn for promo)

## Key Activities
- Agent development and maintenance
- User onboarding and support
- Marketing and beta acquisition
- Billing and subscription management
- Demo showcases and iterations

## Key Resources
- OpenClaw workspace and tools
- AI models and APIs
- Landing page and demo agents
- Community/marketing channels
- Scott's network (Mountain Oyster Club, personal)

## Value Propositions
- Ready-to-use AI agents for everyday tasks (wine recs, scheduling, property search)
- $29/mo affordable access - no setup hassle
- Customizable, powerful (powered by top models)
- MVP beta with direct support from creator

## Customer Relationships
- Self-service signup via landing
- Discord/Telegram community for beta users
- Personalized onboarding calls
- Email support

## Channels
- Landing page (genesis.ai or similar)
- X/LinkedIn promo
- Product Hunt launch
- Referrals from demos

## Customer Segments
- Busy professionals (scheduling)
- Wine enthusiasts (sommelier)
- Home buyers/renters (property)
- Early AI adopters seeking AaS

## Revenue Streams
- Subscriptions: Starter $29/mo (3 agents, 100 queries/day)
- Pro $79/mo (unlimited, custom agents)
- Enterprise custom
- Goal: $1K MRR beta (34 Starter subs)

## Cost Structure
- AI API costs (~$0.50-2/user/mo)
- Hosting (~$20/mo)
- Marketing (organic + $100 ads)
- Time (Scott/Djinni)
